Get Invovlved

MUSIC

Serving in the choir unit of a church is more than singing — it is a sacred calling and a powerful form of ministry. When you join the choir, you become part of a spiritual team tasked with leading God's people into His presence through worship. It's not just about having a beautiful voice; it's about offering your gift to honor the One who gave it.

PROTOCOL

Serving as a protocol officer in church is a unique and honorable ministry. It's more than just welcoming guests or arranging seats — it's a form of worship through excellence, order, and hospitality. Protocol officers are often the first point of contact for visitors and ministers, and their role sets the tone for the entire service.

SANCTUARY

Being a sanctuary keeper in church is a powerful and often overlooked ministry. It is not just about cleaning or arranging chairs — it is about honoring God by maintaining the holiness and orderliness of His house. Just as priests in the Old Testament kept the temple in perfect condition, today's sanctuary keepers serve with the same reverence and dedication.

USHERING

An usher is called to serve with a smile, humility, and attentiveness. Whether it's greeting members, helping first-time visitors, maintaining order during services, or supporting the flow of the program, ushers are essential to ensuring that everything runs smoothly. They are silent ministers who work behind the scenes, but their impact is felt by all.

EVANGELISM

Evangelism is not just about preaching on the streets; it's about carrying the light of Christ into every place we go. Whether it's through one-on-one conversations, outreach programs, community visits, or sharing tracts, evangelism team members are ambassadors of Christ (2 Corinthians 5:20). They represent His love, hope, and saving grace to a world in need.

MEDIA

Serving in the media department is a unique and powerful way to contribute to the work of God in today's digital age. While it may often go unseen by the congregation, the media team plays a vital role in enhancing the worship experience and spreading the gospel beyond the church walls.

PRAYER

Prayer is not just a routine; it is communication with God — the foundation of every true move of God. The prayer department carries the weighty responsibility of interceding for the church, the leaders, the members, the community, and the nations. It is a ministry of standing in the gap (Ezekiel 22:30), warring in the spirit, and creating spiritual atmosphere for revival, healing, breakthrough, and direct

YOUTH FELLOWSHIP

Youth fellowship in church is more than just a gathering — it is a movement of purpose, passion, and spiritual growth. It provides a space where young people can connect, learn, and grow in their relationship with God while discovering their unique identity and calling. In a world full of distractions and pressures, youth fellowship helps to build a solid spiritual foundation. Youth fellowship also develops leadership, service, and responsibility.The energy, creativity, and zeal of the youth are powerful tools in God's hands.
